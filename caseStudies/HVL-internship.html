HVL

At Harmony Venture Labs (HVL), our belief is that technology platforms can and should be used to benefit people and their communities and that new venture creation is a powerful vehicle to deliver those benefits. We are drawn to big ideas and to founders who are bold enough to bring their ideas to life.

Founded in 2020, Harmony Venture Labs is a venture studio and venture equity firm that builds, acquires, and advises SaaS and digital platforms. We champion consistent growth through concerted effort. Our goal is to help companies, advisory clients and our employees navigate the startup world and each stage with intentional momentum toward a desired outcome.

My Experience at HVL

At HVL, I had the opportunity to work in both the studio and advisory teams, where I was able to work on and observe many different aspects like mockup design, graphic design, web design, and UX research. As a part of the studio team, I worked on an internal studio project from its early stages to its launch. The studio project launched as Wellnest, a platform that connects unused gym spaces to service providers.
